The Core Challenge: A Pathway Blocked

The primary obstacle for creative professionals (Marketing, Design) is that their occupations are not listed on the Western Australian Skilled Migration Occupation List (WASMOL). This makes a direct application through the standard State Nominated Migration Program (SNMP) impossible.
