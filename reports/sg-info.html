A 5-Year Actionable Roadmap

This timeline provides a clear, step-by-step guide from diagnosis to your child's successful transition into primary school, helping you stay organized and proactive.

Year 1 (Age 4): Foundation Building

Submit EIPIC application, join parent support groups, maximize CDA contributions, and start private therapy while waiting for a placement.

Years 2-3 (Ages 5-6): EIPIC & P1 Planning

Actively participate in the EIPIC program. Begin researching primary school pathways and attend open houses. Discuss options with your EIPIC team.

Years 4-5 (Ages 7-8): School Transition

Support your child's transition to Primary 1. Build a strong relationship with the school team and begin long-term financial planning with SNTC and the CPF Board.
