An Actionable Roadmap to Nomination

Western Australia Alternative Pathway

The Core Challenge: Pathway Blocked

The primary obstacle for creative professionals (Marketing, Design) is that their occupations are not listed on the Western Australian Skilled Migration Occupation List (WASMOL). This creates a fundamental eligibility barrier for direct application through the standard State Nominated Migration Program (SNMP).
